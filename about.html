Why KumquatNinja?

KumquatNinja was the Xbox GamerTag I created when I was 14 years old. I played a lot of Halo with my friends and brother and meanwhile the name stuck. It was how I chose to represent myself to the world back then and it's now a reminder to not grow up too fast.
