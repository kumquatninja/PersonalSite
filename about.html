Why KumquatNinja?

KumquatNinja was the Xbox GamerTag I created when I was 14 years old. I played a lot of Halo with my brother and friends and meanwhile the name stuck. It was how I chose to represent myself to the world back then and it's now a reminder of the things I will always hold most dear.
