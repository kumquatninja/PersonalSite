This website is a testament to my DIY self-learning hacker artist ethos. I decided to create a platform from scratch, without the use of a framework.

Why KumquatNinja?

KumquatNinja was the Xbox GamerTag I created when I was 14 years old.
I have amazing memories of playing Halo with my friends and brother on it and the name stuck. It was how I chose to represent myself to the world back then and it's now a reminder to not grow up too fast.
